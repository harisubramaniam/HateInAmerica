Hate crimes reported by universities since 2006

The number of reported campus hate incidents increased from 74 in 2006 to 1,300 in 2016. More campuses have created bias-response teams in the past decade, which provide a place for campus community members to file reports after experiencing hate or bias.

SOURCE: U.S. Department of Education, Office of Postsecondary Education, Campus Safety and Security (CSS) survey.
