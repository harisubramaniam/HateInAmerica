News 21 monitors social media activity of far-right leaders

News21 tracked posts of Alex Jones, Billy Roper, Christopher Cantwell, David Duke, James Allsup, Jason Kessler, Pamela Geller, Patrick Little, Peter Brimelow and Richard Spencer on Facebook, GAB, Twitter, YouTube, VK, and WrongThink between June 10 and June 24. Separation of migrant children from parents generated the most posts from them.

SOURCE: News21 analysis of far-right leaders usage of popular and emerging social media platforms.
